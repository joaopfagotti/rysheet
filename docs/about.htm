About Us

Our project is non-profit making!

Our project, Rysheet Depository, is dedicated to preserving the digital heritage of retro games. We collect, archive, and celebrate the rich history of classic games, bringing together stories, reviews, and downloadable content for enthusiasts and newcomers alike.

Founded in 2024, Rysheet Depository began as a passion project among retro gamers and has evolved into a community-driven archive that honors the legacy of the games that defined a generation.

Our mission is to keep nostalgia alive while making these classics accessible for future generations, ensuring that the art and creativity behind retro gaming is never forgotten.

Our main purpose is not to support piracy, but to preserve old games so that future generations can have access to these media. The original publishers of these projects no longer make them available, either for purchase or download, our effort is to ensure that they do not become a "lost media" and that they are forever remembered and celebrated, because whether you want or not, they are ways of expressing art, so look at our project as a large digital museum that has great care for all the items in its collection.
